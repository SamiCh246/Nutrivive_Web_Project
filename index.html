Impact of Hunger

Impact of hunger on children

Children who suffer from malnutrition are more likely to experience stunted growth, delayed cognitive development, and increased risk of infections.

Impact of hunger on women

Women who are malnourished during pregnancy are more likely to experience complications during childbirth and have children with low birth weights.

Where can this problem have lasting effects?

Hunger can lead to social and economic instability, especially in developing countries. It can also exacerbate conflicts and displacement.
